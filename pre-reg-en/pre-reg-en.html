Class System


This registration campaign consists of a 3-class reward system, where participants have a chance to win prizes just by simply registering.

Each class has a separate prize drawing that is initially locked until a specific milestone of registered participants is reached. Reaching a milestone will complete the respective class and unlock the class’s prize and prize drawing.

The registration milestone for each class is as follows:

S Class – 5,000 registered participants
A Class – 20,000 registered participants
B Class – 100,000 registered participants

Upon completion of a class, only those participants that have registered by the milestone are eligible and will be automatically entered into the drawing for that class’s prize!

Those participants that are eligible for an earlier class are also considered in later classes. For example, participants eligible for S Class are also automatically eligible for A Class and B Class when they are unlocked. So register before others for multiple chances to win!

The prize for each class is a surprise and will be revealed upon completion of the previous class.

(NOTE: The S Class prize will be revealed after achieving 2,000 registered participants.)

Prizes correspond to the class level and will increase in value accordingly.

 

Pre-Registration Rules


  • All sections of our Rules & Regulations are in effect.
  • All unlocked class drawings will be performed 1 month after the registration target is met.
  • One (1) winner will be chosen at random out of the eligible registered participants for each unlocked class drawing and will be notified via email.
  • A registered participant may win the prize of multiple class drawings as long as the registered participant has met the eligibility requirements to enter each class drawing.
  • Caution! Registration is only requested once and is limited to ONLY ONE registration per person via one email address. A violation of this rule disqualifies the participant permanently and he forfeits any and all benefits and prizes he may have won.
  • Participation is open to all individuals who are at the time of their participation at least 18 years old and who have their residence in Japan.
  • Winners may be requested to prove their identity with a valid copy of their driver’s license or other government issued identity card.
  • Prizes from the class drawings are not transferable to another party prior to issuance.
