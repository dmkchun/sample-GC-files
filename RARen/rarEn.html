Rules and Regulations

1. Scope
1.1. The following provisions apply for the Gotchamp service of GK Gotchamp, Yokohama, Naka-ku, Minami-nakadori 4-43, represented by the managing director Hans-Henning Judek, hereinafter referred to as "GK Gotchamp".
1.2. In the free game Gotchamp the participant first selects 6 numbers between 1 and 49, and then selects a Super number from the remaining 43 numbers. The participant is eligible to win prizes based on the amount of matches between the selected numbers and the numbers drawn in the next Gotchamp game drawing.
1.3. A participant takes part in the free Gotchamp game by fulfilling all the requirements. By participating in the Gotchamp game the participant agrees with the following regulations:


2. Participation and Winning Rights
2.1. Participation in the Gotchamp game presupposes that the participant has previously registered with GK Gotchamp on the website of the Gotchamp game. Registration is only requested once and is limited to ONLY ONE registration per person via one email address. A violation of this rule disqualifies the participant permanently and he forfeits any and all benefits and prizes he may have won.
2.2. Participation is open to all individuals who are at the time of their participation at least 18 years old and residing in Japan.
2.3. To become eligible for participation in the game, it is absolutely necessary that all personal data submitted are complete and truthful.
2.4. A winning participant of a Gotchamp game only has a right to the prize according to the provisions in Sections 4 and 5 of these Rules and Regulations. The participant must, at the time of his participation in the Gotchamp game, fulfill all requirements and data provided during the registration process. If the participant is no longer a resident of Japan, he forfeits any claims to prizes in the Gotchamp game.
2.5. Participation and the chances of winning at Gotchamp are independent in any way of the purchase of goods or the use of paid or unpaid services by GK Gotchamp or other companies related to the organization.


3. Exclusion from the Gotchamp game
3.1. Legal representatives and employees of the GK Gotchamp, and its subsidiaries, and their relatives living with them under the same roof, are excluded from participating in the Gotchamp game.
3.2. In the case of a breach of these Rules and Regulations, GK Gotchamp reserves the right to exclude individuals from participating; especially in the case of multiple participations and participants submitting false or incomplete personal data.
3.3. Also excluded are individuals who use unauthorized aids or otherwise gain advantages through manipulation. This includes the participation in lottery clubs and automated services, e.g. through the use of scripts or robots, as well as game participation via straw men. In such cases prizes won may be subsequently denied and reclaimed.


4. Implementation of Gotchamp
4.1. The Gotchamp game is exclusively organized and carried out by GK Gotchamp.
4.2. The Gotchamp game is offered by GK Gotchamp as a common free sweepstake for the benefit of developing charities as well as domestic and international renewable energy and other high CSR value projects.
4.3. For the daily draw the participant logs into his account on https://www.gotchamp.jp/ ("Gotchamp page") and first selects 6 numbers between 1 and 49, and then selects a Super number from the remaining 43 numbers. No numbers may be repeated. For this the participant clicks on seven numbers in the selection field, indicated by a change in color. After selecting the desired numbers, the participant clicks play and then confirms his selection by clicking on one of the displayed banner ads. The decision to select a particular banner ad does not influence the chances for winning. The selection input screen on the Gotchamp page changes to the confirmation screen, indicating that the selection was accepted.
4.4. Each selection is stored by GK Gotchamp. Each participant may take part in the daily draws only once per account. Multiple participation attempts for the daily draw are rejected. Number selections received after proper login procedures have been accomplished are understood as to have been made by the participant, even if a third party gained access to the account of the participant.
4.5. From the Launch Date a drawing is performed on each day at 18:02 Japan time. This drawing considers all participants that have submitted their number selections up until 18:00 Japan time on the Gotchamp page. The official time of submission is documented in the play confirmation email. Submissions that do not reach GK Gotchamp by the specified time will be automatically considered as participation for the drawing on the following day. Submissions that are not correctly filled out are excluded from the drawing.
4.6. If the selection of a participant matches the seven winning numbers (six numbers between 1 and 49 and the Super number from the remaining 43 numbers) the participant wins the grand prize. In case that several participants have met the criteria for winning the grand prize, the grand prize will be split among the winners according to the provisions of Section 5. All participants, who have selected (excluding the Super number) six, five or four numbers, conforming to the winning numbers drawn out of 1 to 49, will win prizes.
4.7. Winners are individually responsible for paying any applicable taxes.
4.8. Complaints have to be filed in writing to GK Gotchamp at support@gotchamp.com within 14 calendar days after becoming aware of the reason for the complaint and state the daily game number. Complaints by phone or late complaints will not be processed.


5. Prizes
5.1. Tier definition based on winning criteria:
Tier 1 – grand prize; match 6 numbers out of 6 and match Super number
Tier 2 – match 6 numbers out of 6 (Super number does not match)
Tier 3 – match 5 numbers out of 6 (excluding the Super number)
Tier 4 – match 4 numbers out of 6 (excluding the Super number)
5.2. The Tier 1, grand prize is Japanese Yen (JPY) 1 million.
5.3. The grand prize will be paid in 10 monthly installments of JPY 100,000 at the end of the month.
5.4. The winner cannot request payment of the grand prize in a lump sum. The claim to the prize is inheritable.
5.5. The Tier 2 prize for six correct winning numbers from the number block of 1 to 49 is JPY 100,000; the Tier 3 prize for five correct numbers is JPY 5,000; and the Tier 4 prize for four correct winning numbers is JPY 500. If there are several winners in a particular prize category, each of them will receive the full prize amount for the respective prize category.
5.6. Participants that have not won any prize in a calendar month are automatically entered into the “Booby Prize” drawing. The prize for the "Booby Prize" drawing is announced in advance on the Gotchamp website.
5.7. Participants that have participated in the Gotchamp game at least 10 times in a calendar month are automatically entered into the “Participation” drawing. The prize for the “Participation” drawing is announced in advance on the Gotchamp website.
5.8. Participants that have participated in the Gotchamp game on at least 8 weekend days in a calendar month are automatically entered into the “Loyal Weekender” drawing. The prize for the “Loyal Weekender” drawing is announced in advance on the Gotchamp website.
5.9. The GK Gotchamp can pledge additional goods or cash prices for special draws in the form of goods, vouchers or cash prizes. Information about these special draws, the respective rules and regulations, the vouchers and the arrangements for redemption will be published by GK Gotchamp on the Gotchamp website.
5.10. Prizes up to JPY 100,000 can be distributed as non-cash prizes at the discretion of GK Gotchamp. Winners have no claim to cash payouts. Warranty and liability claims for non-cash prizes are exclusively responsibility of the manufacturer according to the Rules and Regulations and can therefore be claimed exclusively to the merchant.


6. Announcement of Winners
6.1. The winning numbers of the previous day are announced on the Gotchamp website so that the participants can verify for themselves whether they have won. Prizes are not transferrable to another party prior to issuance. GK Gotchamp does not guarantee accuracy of the announcement.
6.2. GK Gotchamp will notify the winners via email. The Tier 3 prize, Tier 4 prize, Loyal Weekender prize and Booby Prize will be sent to the winner within 15 business days.
6.3. All other winners have to claim their prizes within three months after the announcement of the results on the Gotchamp website and notify GK Gotchamp. Winners must prove their identity with a copy of their driver's license or identity card. Minors will receive the Prize only if their legal guardian submits a copy of a valid identification document, authorizes the participation of the minor in the game according these Rules and Regulations, and agrees to the receipt of the Prize. Excluding the Tier 1 prize, all other prizes will be sent to the winner within 15 business days of identity confirmation.
6.4. If the winner does not respond within three months of notification of the winning numbers on the Gotchamp website and informs GK Gotchamp, he forfeits the Prize. No other participant has the right to claim the Prize.


7. Notifications, Mailings and Newsletters
7.1. The participant will receive automatically generated emails for the following cases: (1) registration confirmation; (2) number selection confirmation; (3) reset password verification; and (4) winning notification.
7.2. The participant may receive from the start, digital newsletters and emails with information from GK Gotchamp, regarding special events, charities and projects.
