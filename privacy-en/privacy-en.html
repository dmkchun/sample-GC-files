3.  About cookies
  In principle, cookies may be used in this site for efficient site navigation, user authentication and login information retention (via use of "Remember Me").
  A cookie is a small text file, stored on the participant's computer upon visiting this site, which contains personal information, recorded as arbitrary characters, and data about the exchange of information between this site's web server and the participant's web browsing software (browser). The next time the same page is accessed, this site can use the information stored in the cookie to customize the display for each individual participant.
  If the participant has authorized the transmission of cookies in his browser settings, the website can acquire the cookie from the participant's browser. In addtion, for privacy protection, the participant's browser only sends the cookies that were received by the website's server.
  To generally find out more about cookies, click here.
  Depending on the settings of the participant's browser, it is possible to indicate whether or not to receive and accept cookies from a site. Please note that if the participant configures his settings to not use cookies, he will not receive services that require authentication and various services on the internet may be limited.

However, this information will not be used with the intent to link personal information. But then again, in the case of an occurrence of "spam posts" to the blog or other areas, we may use IP addresses, etc. for tracking the source of such spam.
