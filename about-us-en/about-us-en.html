Hans-Henning Judek, Founder & CEO, 
is an environmental entrepreneur and Top Innovation Prize winner at the Asian Clean Energy Forum (ACEF2008). He is a German citizen that has been living in Japan for 35 years and working all over Asia in the field of renewable energy (solar, biomass, bio fuel). He is registered consultant to the Asian Development Bank and a regular speaker at the ACEF.
